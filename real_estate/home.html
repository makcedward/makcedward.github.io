Audience

The target audience includes investors and potential homeowners. Different senses provide visualization for seeing thins from a different angle. The State view provides an overview of Zillow Home Value Index (ZHVI) changing in the last 20 years. The Metropolitan view focuses on comparing the United States and metropolitans. The Market Analysis view provides insight to investors and potential homeowners when should we buy a house.

Data

Primary data source is Zillow public data. It includes Zillow Home Value Index (ZHVI), Zillow Observed Rent Index (ZORI), For-Sale Inventory, Mean and Median Days to Pending and Median Sale Price. Second data source is gross domestic product (GDP) which can be retrieved from Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis.
