Audience

The target audience include investors and potential home owners. Different senses provide a visualization for seeing thins from a different angle. State view provides an overview of Zillow Home Value Index (ZHVI) changing in last 20 years. Metropolitan view focuses on comparing among United States and metropolitans. Market Analysis provides an insight to investors and potentail home owners when should we buy a house.

Data

Primary data source is Zillow public data. It includes Zillow Home Value Index (ZHVI), Zillow Observed Rent Index (ZORI), For-Sale Inventory, Mean and Median Days to Pending and Median Sale Price. Second data source is gross domestic product (GDP) which can be retrieved from Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis.
